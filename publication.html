I Wait for You at Sunrise

And at the edge of the world, there is a fire. Gold, pink, orange, it spreads its flaming fingersand wakes up the darkness, envelops the whole skywith a joyous embrace. The rain pours down in heavy deluge, and stops after a while. Grief can only last for so long.

- Emily Skahill

Ode to Vienna

I will surrender my white flag in many wars

I will scream as the rose pierces my side -

Unable to move my bloodied palm off the grass it rests upon.

But I will not give you the satisfaction of my treaty.

Do not tell me of my losses,

It’s over, time to raise that old flag, Do not say Vienna waits

Because that is not yours to say.

- Sheena Holt.
