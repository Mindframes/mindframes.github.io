About

We’re Mindframes, the literary magazine club at Saint Francis High School in Mountain View, California. We host a variety of activities from Cafe Mots to literature movie night. Our annual publication, Mindframes, is student-made, original literary magazine that features fine arts, both digital and traditional, poems, and even short prose fictions.
